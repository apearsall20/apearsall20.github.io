Historical Home Price Data

It is well documented in the news that people in the younger generations are having increasing difficulty buying their first homes. Data shows that the median home price has gone up 13X in the past 50 years. Please hit the next button to see how that compares to wages or inflation.

Home prices have gone up around 13X while inflation has only taken other costs of living up around 6X. The inflation adjusted wages have barely gone up in the past half century. The stock market can be seen to increase at about the same rate as home prices. Having money is the best way to make money in the US.

Hover over any state to see how the state's median price per home has changed over the past 50 years. Click on a state to zoom into the county level. And hover over the county to see that counties trend.
